Meridian Systems Reports First Quarter Fiscal 2026 Results

PALO ALTO, Calif. — Meridian Systems, Inc. (NASDAQ: MRDN) today reported financial results for the first quarter of fiscal 2026 ended March 31, 2026. Total revenue was $1,241.3 million, up 18% year over year. GAAP diluted EPS was $0.87 and non-GAAP diluted EPS was $1.12.

Guidance

For the second quarter of fiscal 2026, the company expects total revenue in the range of $1.31 to $1.34 billion. Meridian expects second-quarter non-GAAP diluted EPS of $1.15 to $1.20. The company anticipates operating margin of approximately 23% for the second quarter.

For the full year fiscal 2026, Meridian now expects total revenue of $5.40 to $5.55 billion.
